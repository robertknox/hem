Robert Selkirk Knox

 

 

Robert Selkirk Knox is a Califonia based writer and the author of Sirens In The Ferns.

The story revolves around disappearing strippers, syringe toting bad guys, a remote cabin tucked away in the redwoods where a young girl is being held captive and a fern garden where rows of rectangular mounds of earth prutrude up from beneath the fronds and shadows of the forest.

The first chapter is available on this web site under the fiction menu item.
